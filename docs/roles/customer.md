# Customer Portal Guide

This guide is for external customers who access uqmes to view their order status and documents.

    - Certificates of Conformance for completed orders
    - Notification preferences for order status changes
    - RBAC in action: Only Midwest Fleet orders visible, not other customers' data

## Your Access

As a Customer user, you can:

- View your company's orders
- Track order progress
- Access shared documents
- View part information

You have read-only access limited to your company's data.

## Getting Started

### First-Time Login

1. You'll receive an invitation email
2. Click the link to set up your account
3. Create a password (or use SSO if available)
4. Log in to the portal

### Portal Overview

When you log in, you'll see:

- **Tracker**: Your orders and their status

Documents for your orders are accessible from within the order details on Tracker.

## Viewing Your Orders

### Order List

1. Navigate to **Tracker**
2. See all your company's orders
3. Each card shows:
   - Order number
   - Progress percentage
   - Due date
   - Status

### Order Progress

The progress bar shows completion:

| Progress | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| 0-25% | Early stage |
| 25-50% | In production |
| 50-75% | Progressing |
| 75-99% | Nearing completion |
| 100% | Complete |

### Order Details

Click an order to see:

- Order information
- Part list and status
- Associated documents
- Key dates

## Understanding Status

### Order Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **In Progress** | Work is underway |
| **Complete** | All parts finished |
| **On Hold** | Temporarily paused |
| **Shipped** | Order has shipped |

### Part Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **In Process** | Part moving through production |
| **Complete** | Part finished |
| **Quality Hold** | Under quality review |

## Accessing Documents

### Available Documents

You can access documents shared with you:

- Certificates of Conformance
- Test reports
- Shipping documents
- Product documentation

### Viewing Documents

1. Open an order from **Tracker**
2. Navigate to the **Documents** section within the order
3. Click document to view
4. Download if needed

### Document Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Certificate** | Quality certification |
| **Report** | Test or inspection results |
| **Specification** | Product specs (if shared) |

## Searching

### Finding Orders

Use the search bar:
- Search by order number
- Search by part number
- Search by PO reference

### Filtering

Use filters to narrow results:
- By status
- By date range

## Notifications

You may receive notifications for:

- Order status changes
- Documents shared with you
- Order completion
- Shipping updates

Configure preferences in your profile.

## Your Profile

### Accessing Profile

Click your name/avatar in the header, then **Profile**.

### Profile Settings

- Update contact information
- Change password (if not SSO)
- Set notification preferences

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I track a specific order?

Use the search bar on Tracker with your order number or PO reference.

### Why can't I see certain information?

You only have access to information your supplier has shared with you. Internal production details are not visible.

### How do I get more documents?

Contact your supplier representative to request additional documentation.

### Who do I contact with questions?

Contact your supplier's customer service or sales representative for order-specific questions.

### Can I update order information?

No, the portal is read-only. Contact your supplier to request changes.

## What You Can't Do

As a customer portal user:

- You cannot edit orders or parts
- You cannot see internal quality issues
- You cannot access other customers' data
- You cannot create documents or records
